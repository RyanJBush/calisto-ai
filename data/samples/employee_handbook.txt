Acme Corp Employee Handbook (Excerpt)

1. Working Hours and Remote Work
Full-time employees are expected to work 40 hours per week. Core collaboration
hours are 10:00 AM to 3:00 PM in the employee's primary timezone. Remote work
is permitted up to three days per week with manager approval.

2. Paid Time Off
Employees accrue 15 days of paid time off (PTO) per year during their first
two years of employment, increasing to 20 days thereafter. PTO requests must
be submitted at least two weeks in advance for absences longer than one day.

3. Expense Reimbursement
Business expenses are reimbursed within 10 business days after receipt
submission through the company expense portal. Meals during business travel
are reimbursed up to $75 per day. Receipts are required for any single
expense above $25.

4. Information Security
All employees must complete annual security training. Customer data must
never be stored on personal devices. Access to production systems requires
manager approval and is reviewed quarterly.
