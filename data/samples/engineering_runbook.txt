Acme Platform Engineering Runbook (Excerpt)

Deployment Process
All production deployments go through the standard CI/CD pipeline. Changes
must pass unit tests, integration tests, and security scans before being
promoted to staging. Deploys to production require approval from a second
engineer.

On-Call Rotations
The platform team operates a 24/7 on-call rotation. Primary on-call holds
the pager for one week starting Mondays at 10:00 AM ET. Secondary on-call
is engaged when the primary does not acknowledge within 10 minutes.

Service Level Objectives
The API service targets 99.9% monthly availability and a p95 latency under
300 ms for read endpoints. Error budget burn rate exceeding 2x triggers a
review of recent changes.

Database Migrations
Schema migrations are managed with Alembic. Forward and reverse migrations
must both be tested in staging. Long-running migrations on tables larger
than 1 million rows must use the online migration playbook.
